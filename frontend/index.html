    About AI Pulse

    Global AI Intelligence
    on autopilot.

    AI Pulse is an autonomous intelligence platform that continuously monitors, filters, and synthesizes artificial intelligence news from across the web — delivering what matters, stripped of noise.

    The Problem
    AI moves faster than anyone can track manually.

    Hundreds of papers, product launches, funding announcements, and regulatory changes happen every day. Important signals are buried in noise. Non-AI content floods general news feeds. Staying informed requires hours of manual curation.

    How It Works

    The intelligence pipeline

    1
    Fetch
    NewsAPI queries and arXiv categories are polled every 10 minutes, pulling hundreds of articles from across the web.
    2
    Filter
    Keyword pre-filtering removes non-AI content. Only articles containing verified AI signals advance to the next stage.
    3
    Analyze
    A language model classifies each article, generates a structured summary, assigns priority, and detects cross-article trends.
    4
    Deliver
    Processed intelligence is stored and served in real time. The dashboard auto-refreshes every 30 seconds.
    Data Sources
    Every major source in AI, automatically tracked.

    AI Pulse monitors a curated set of high-quality news sources and academic repositories, filtering exclusively for artificial intelligence content. Non-AI articles are rejected at two layers: keyword analysis and LLM validation.

    AI Processing
    LLM-powered analysis for every article.

    Each batch of articles is sent to a language model (Ollama locally, or Groq/OpenAI in the cloud). The model classifies the article, writes a structured summary, assigns an impact statement, flags high-priority items, and identifies trending topics across the batch.

    Supported providers: Ollama (local, free), Groq (free tier), OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini). Configurable via the .env file.
